cairo airport, 1963, 25,000,000P

some informations about cairo airport:

It is an international airport located about 22 kilometers from the center of Cairo, the capital of Egypt, in the northeastern direction. The airport’s land area is about 40 million square meters, and it is considered the air gateway to Egypt and the continent of Africa. The airport is the second largest airport on the continent in terms of congestion and passenger density.

sharm airport, 1968, 10,000,000P

some informations about sharm airport:

It is an international airport 23 km away from Sharm El-Sheikh. It was formerly known as Al-Afeira International Airport. It is one of the giant tourist airports. It was handed over to the Egyptian Civil Aviation Authority (currently the Ministry of Civil Aviation) after the rest of the occupied territories were recovered from Sinai In April 1982, it was managed civilianly

sphinx airport, 2016, 20,000,000P

some informations about sphinx airport:

It is an international airport established in 2016 in Giza, west of Cairo, Egypt. It is located on the Cairo-Alexandria Desert Road at Kilometer 45. The airport was established with the aim of relieving pressure on Cairo International Airport and raising pressure and density on the heart of Cairo, as this airport serves the Sheikh Zayed and 6th October.
